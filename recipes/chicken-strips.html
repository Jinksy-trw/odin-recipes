Chicen Strips

>

Air fryer cooked chicken strips are a quick and easy to make meal. You will love it, and your family too.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of panko bread crubms
  • 2 table spoons of canola oil
  • 8 chicken tenders
  • freshly chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients seperatly.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat basket with cooking spray
  3. Whisk an egg in a small bowl. In another bowl stir together panko and canola oil until loose and crumbly
  4. Dip each chicken tender into egg, allowing excess to drip off. Dip chicken in panko mixture to coat completely, press the breadcrumbs into tenders with your hand so it sticks to it.
  5. Arrange chicken in an even layer in the air fryer basket. Cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 12 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted
    into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot garnished with freshly chopped parsley. Good Appetite!
